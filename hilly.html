Hilly Run

Your starting point for this run is: New Weir car park, Symonds Yat East, Ross-on-Wye, HR9 6BY, United Kingdom

Run route recommendation: From this car park next to the river, you can run along the river and take a left up a marked footpath after approximately 100m to start the long climb all the way up to the Symonds Yat viewpoint. With stunning vistas providing a great reward before you can also enjoy one of the many paths to descend on.
